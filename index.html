Our services

Weight Gain

Weight gain is an increase in body weight. This can involve an increase in muscle mass, fat deposits, excess fluids such as water or other factors. Weight gain can be a symptom of a serious medical condition.

Weight Loss

Weight loss is a decrease in body weight. This can occur due to various factors, including a reduction in muscle mass, fat loss, or loss of body fluids. Consultation with a healthcare professional is recommended for significant weight changes.

Strength Training

Strength training is a form of exercise that uses resistance to induce muscular contraction, building strength, endurance, and muscle mass. It plays a crucial role in overall fitness and can contribute to weight management and other health benefits.

Training and Shaping

Training and shaping involve physical activities aimed at improving fitness, body composition, and overall well-being. These activities may include cardiovascular exercises, strength training, and flexibility exercises.

Silambam

Silambam is a traditional Indian martial art that incorporates the use of staffs or sticks. It has historical and cultural significance and provides physical and mental benefits, including improved coordination and flexibility.

Self-Defense

Self-defense involves techniques and strategies to protect oneself from physical harm. Learning self-defense can empower individuals, enhance confidence, and contribute to personal safety and well-being.
